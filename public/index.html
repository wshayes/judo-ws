Overview

This is an adults only Judo club - you must be 16 years or older.

Mind

Develop your mind while exercising. We consider many aspects of judo training - moving meditation.

Body

Judo improves balance, core strength and overall fitness. It also teaches you how to fall really well :) - and since we are much more likely to fall than be attacked, falling well is an important life skill especially as we get older.

Mutual Benefit

Mutual Benefit and Welfare is a cornerstone of Judo carried down from the founder Jigoro Kano. In caring for others, you care for yourself.
